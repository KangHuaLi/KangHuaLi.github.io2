Easy-Guide: A Simple Smart Phonebased Guiding System for Visually Impaired People

This is my graduate project in my university. Our group provide the guiding system for visually impaired people by the combination of smart phone and deep learning. The phone keeps capturing the front image and sends to the remote computer to do the recognition. It will transmit the result back to the phone, and tells the user by voice.
